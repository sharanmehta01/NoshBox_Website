How It Works

1. Restaurants List Surplus

Partner restaurants and food outlets can upload surplus food in real-time, offering it at a discounted rate.

2. Customers Browse & Purchase

Eco-conscious consumers, students, and budget-savvy individuals explore discounted meal options and place their orders.

3. Pickup & Reduce Waste

Customers collect their orders at designated times, preventing waste while enjoying delicious meals at unbeatable prices.
